# AI Prompt Marketplace

**Decentralized marketplace for AI prompts, datasets & fine-tune data on Shelby (Aptos blockchain)**

Upload your best prompts, datasets or AI training materials → store on Shelby's high-speed decentralized hot storage → set price → earn micropayments every time someone reads/uses your data.

AI devs & creators can discover, buy with APT micro-fees, and access instantly with sub-second reads. All verifiable on-chain, no middlemen, full ownership & royalty built-in.

Built for the AI + Web3 era in 2026.
